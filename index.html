About us


Since its inception in 2016, the Big Data Analytics program (Or, popularly known as Data Science) ,RKMVERI has consistently produced numerous talents both in academia and industrial sectors. The program was started in collaboration with Tata Consultancy Services and has been functioning under the Dept. of Computer Science, RKMVERI. Equipped with expert faculty, advanced laboratories, updated course curriculum, our students acquire the demanding skill-sets both for industry and academics. Our students come from various backgrounds (e.g. Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science, Physics, Economics etc.) and are highly motivated towards working in the Analytics domain.
